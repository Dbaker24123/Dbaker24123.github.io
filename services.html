Our Services

School Cyber Program offers a variety of services to help our students succeed both in and out of the classroom.

Career Services

Our career services department provides our students with resources and support to help them find internships and job opportunities in the field of cyber security. We offer career counseling, resume building workshops, and networking events with industry professionals.

Academic Support

We offer a variety of academic support services to help our students succeed in their coursework. These services include tutoring, study groups, and academic advising.

Research Opportunities

School Cyber Program is committed to advancing the field of cyber security through cutting-edge research. We offer our students opportunities to participate in research projects and collaborate with our faculty on research initiatives.

Professional Development

We believe that continuing education is essential for success in the constantly evolving field of cyber security. We offer professional development workshops and seminars to help our students stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies.
