Courses

Our courses are designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills they need to stay safe and secure online. We offer the following courses:

Cyber Security Basics

This course provides an introduction to the basics of cyber security, including how to identify common threats and vulnerabilities, how to protect personal and sensitive information, and how to stay safe online.

  • Duration: 4 weeks
  • Cost: $199

Advanced Cyber Security

This course covers advanced topics in cyber security, including network security, cryptography, and ethical hacking. Students will learn how to develop and implement effective cyber security strategies and techniques.

  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Cost: $399

Cyber Bullying Prevention

This course is designed to help students understand and prevent cyber bullying. Students will learn how to recognize the signs of cyber bullying, how to respond to cyber bullying incidents, and how to promote a culture of respect and kindness online.

  • Duration: 6 weeks
  • Cost: $299
