About Us

The School Cyber Program was founded in 2010 to promote cyber security education and training in schools. We offer a range of services to help schools and students stay safe online, including:

Service Description
Training for teachers and staff on cyber security best practices Our team provides training and workshops to educate teachers and staff on best practices for staying safe online and protecting sensitive information.
Workshops for students on online safety and cyber bullying prevention We offer workshops to students to educate them about online safety, including how to protect their personal information and prevent cyber bullying.
Assistance with developing and implementing cyber security policies and procedures We work with schools to develop and implement policies and procedures for cyber security that are tailored to their specific needs and requirements.
Consulting services for schools and school districts Our team provides consulting services to schools and school districts to help them identify and address cyber security risks and vulnerabilities.

Our team of cyber security experts has years of experience working in the field and is dedicated to helping schools create a safe and secure online environment for students, staff, and faculty.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower schools to take control of their cyber security by providing education, training, and support. We believe that every student, staff member, and faculty member has the right to a safe and secure online environment, and we are committed to helping schools achieve that goal.
