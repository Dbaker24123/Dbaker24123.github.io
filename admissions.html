Admissions

Thank you for considering School Cyber Program. We are dedicated to providing our students with the best possible education and training in the field of cyber security.

Application Process

  1. Complete and submit the online application form.
  2. Submit official transcripts from all colleges and universities attended.
  3. Submit a current resume.
  4. Submit a personal statement outlining your interest in cyber security and your career goals.

Tuition and Financial Aid

Tuition for our programs varies based on the length of the program and other factors. Please contact our admissions office for more information on tuition rates and payment options.

We offer a variety of financial aid options to our students, including scholarships, grants, and loans. Please contact our financial aid office for more information on financial aid options and how to apply.

Admissions Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree from an accredited institution
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Strong background in computer science or related field
  • Minimum TOEFL score of 80 (for international students only)
